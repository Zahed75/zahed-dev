Project Overview

POC Academy is a fast-growing EdTech platform in Bangladesh focused on providing accessible, high-quality education to students across the country. The organization wanted to digitize its examination process by developing a comprehensive internal web application that could handle online exams for thousands of students at once.

The project's vision was to create a reliable, scalable, and user-friendly examination system that could seamlessly serve both students and administrators, while maintaining transparency and real-time analytics.
